# Weekly Breakdown: A 13-Week Curriculum

This document provides a sample 13-week curriculum for a course based on this textbook.

### Week 1: Introduction to Physical AI
- **Chapter 1: What is Physical AI**
- Activity: Identifying Physical AI in the Real World

### Week 2: Humanoid Robotics Foundations
- **Chapter 2: Humanoid Robotics Foundations**
- Activity: Design Your Own Humanoid Robot

### Week 3: Sensors and Perception
- **Chapter 3: Sensors & Perception**
- Activity: The "Sensor" in Your Pocket

### Week 4: Introduction to ROS 2
- **Chapter 4: ROS 2 Fundamentals**
- Activity: Creating a Simple Publisher and Subscriber

### Week 5: Simulation Environments
- **Chapter 5: Gazebo & Unity Simulation**
- Activity: Building a Simple Robot in Gazebo

### Week 6: Advanced Simulation with NVIDIA Isaac Sim
- **Chapter 6: NVIDIA Isaac Sim & Isaac ROS**
- Activity: Recreating Your Room in Isaac Sim

### Week 7: Vision-Language-Action (VLA) Models
- **Chapter 7: Vision-Language-Action Robotics**
- Activity: Commanding a "Pick and Place" Robot

### Week 8: Conversational Robotics
- **Chapter 8: Conversational Robotics (GPT + Whisper)**
- Activity: Having a Conversation with Your Robot

### Week 9: Humanoid Locomotion
- **Chapter 9: Humanoid Locomotion & Manipulation** (Part 1)
- Activity: The "Egg Challenge"

### Week 10: Humanoid Manipulation
- **Chapter 9: Humanoid Locomotion & Manipulation** (Part 2)
- Activity: Programming a Grasping Motion

### Week 11: Capstone Project - Design and Build
- **Chapter 10: Capstone: Autonomous Humanoid Robot** (Part 1)
- Students begin designing and building their capstone project robots.

### Week 12: Capstone Project - Software and Integration
- **Chapter 10: Capstone: Autonomous Humanoid Robot** (Part 2)
- Students develop the software for their robots and integrate all the components.

### Week 13: Final Project Demonstrations
- Students present their capstone projects to the class.
