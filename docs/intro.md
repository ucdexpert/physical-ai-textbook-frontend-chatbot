# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook
*A Beginner-Friendly Guide to Building the Future. This book is a beginner-friendly guide to understanding how Artificial Intelligence can move from the digital world into the physical world.
Physical AI means AI that can sense, move, and act in the real world, just like humans and animals do.
Humanoid Robotics focuses on creating robots that look, move, and behave like humans — walking, talking, balancing, understanding their environment, and helping people in daily life.

This guide explains these advanced ideas in simple language, using examples, illustrations, and real-world stories so young learners can easily understand:

how intelligent robots think,

how they learn from their environment,

and how they interact safely with humans.

Through this book, students will explore how Physical AI and Humanoid Robots are becoming the future of schools, hospitals, homes, and space missions — and how they can become the creators of that future.*
